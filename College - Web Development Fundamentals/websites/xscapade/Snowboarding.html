Snowboarding

In snowboarding you get to tear down snowy mountains on a board and get to swerve around obstacles or do tricks.

Boarding is probably one of the coolest sports out there - and not just because it's cold!

Make sure you know your 'death spiral' move from your 'camel spin', because judges award marks for style!
